How to Play

RIVALRY CLUB

Welcome to Rivalry Club!

Challenge your rival with customized, auto-balancing tier lists! Learn this exciting new way to play your favorite fighting game.

1. Create an Account

First, you and your rival must create accounts. Tap "Sign Up" on the authentication screen to get started.

2. Start a Rivalry

After creating your accounts:

• Tap "Create New Rivalry"
• Search for and select your opponent
• Choose your game (currently Super Smash Ultimate)
• Your tier lists start empty, but you can edit immediately

Pro Tip: Type "npc" in the search to instantly find test opponents and try out Rivalry Club solo!

3. Edit Your Tier List

Most tiers (S, A, B, C, D, E) contain 12 characters, while F Tier contains 14 characters. Rank characters from your best in S Tier down to your worst in F Tier.

To edit your tier list:

• Tap "View Tier Lists" on your rivalry page
• Tap "Edit Tier List"
• Tap a character icon, then tap the spot where you want it to go
• Tap "Save List" when finished

4. Play Contests

Boot up Super Smash Ultimate with these settings:

• 3 Stock matches
• Damage Handicap ON
• Any other rules you prefer

In the app:

• Tap "Create new contest"
• You'll each be assigned an unplaced character or a random character from your current tier
• Apply the handicap shown (if any)
• Play the match!
• Return to the app and select the winner and stock difference

5. How Tiers Change

After each contest, players move between tiers based on the stock difference:

1 Stock Victory: Either the winner moves down one tier OR the loser moves up one tier (randomly chosen)
2 Stock Victory: Winner moves down one tier AND loser moves up one tier
3 Stock Victory: Both players move one tier, plus an additional tier move for either winner or loser (randomly chosen)

6. Character Movement Within Tiers

As contests are resolved, characters automatically move up or down within your tier list based on their performance. Winning characters rise in rank, while losing characters fall.

7. Prestige = Bragging Rights

If a player moves below F Tier, he gains a Prestige level (shown as +1, +2, etc.). Each prestige level adds a 20% damage handicap against him.

For example: If a player has +2 prestige over his rival, it means he'll have an approximately equal chance of winning or losing when playing with a 40% damage handicap. These are the bragging rights inherent in prestige!

If his rival thinks he is better, he will have to prove it by winning more to force the prestige down and remove the handicap. The handicap applies until the player is forced back up to his regular tier list.

8. Tips & Features

Shuffle Characters: If you get a character you've banned or don't want to play, tap "Shuffle" to get a new random character from your current tier, or unknown tier, without counting as a contest.
Contest History: View all past contests and see how your rivalry has evolved. You can also undo the most recent contest if needed.

Ready to Play?

Now that you know the rules, create your first rivalry and experience Super Smash Ultimate in a whole new way!
